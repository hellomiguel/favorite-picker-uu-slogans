Picker

Instructions:

When given more than two options you can pick as many as you like. Eventually you'll be shown only two choices. Keep picking until a favorite moves into the, "Found Favorites" section. Keep picking to find your 2nd Favorite, 3rd Favorite, etc.

NB: I've made it so that it'll save your progress so you can leave and come back. This *should* work on all devices/browsers
